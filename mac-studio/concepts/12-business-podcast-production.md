# Business Concept 12: Podcast Production Studio

## The Business
Full-service podcast production: recording, editing, transcription, distribution.

## Hardware
- **Mac Studio M1 Max** (32GB) - editing + AI processing
- **Audio interface**: Focusrite Scarlett / Universal Audio
- **Quality microphones**: Shure SM7B, Rode PodMic
- **Acoustic treatment**: Foam panels, reflection filters

## Revenue Streams
1. **Full production**: €200-500/episode
2. **Editing only**: €50-150/episode
3. **Transcription**: €50-100/episode
4. **Show notes + clips**: €50-100/episode
5. **Monthly retainer**: €500-2000/month per show

## AI-Powered Workflow

### Recording
- Multi-track recording in Logic Pro
- Backup audio capture

### Post-Production (AI-Assisted)
```
Raw audio →
  Noise reduction (AI) →
  Level normalization →
  Remove filler words (AI) →
  Transcribe (Whisper) →
  Generate show notes (LLM) →
  Create audiogram clips →
  Export multiple formats →
  Distribute to platforms
```

### Deliverables Per Episode
- Edited audio (48kHz WAV + MP3)
- Full transcript
- Show notes with timestamps
- 3-5 audiogram clips for social
- YouTube version with waveform

## Software Stack
- **Logic Pro / Descript**: Editing
- **Whisper.cpp**: Transcription
- **Claude/GPT API**: Show notes generation
- **iZotope RX**: Audio repair
- **Headliner**: Audiogram creation
- **ffmpeg**: Audio/video processing

## Time Per Episode
| Task | Manual | AI-Assisted |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Editing | 2-3 hours | 1 hour |
| Transcription | 1-2 hours | 10 minutes |
| Show notes | 30-60 min | 5 minutes |
| Clips | 1 hour | 15 minutes |
| **Total** | **5-7 hours** | **~1.5 hours** |

## Scaling
One Mac Studio can handle:
- 5-10 weekly podcasts (with good workflow)
- Batch processing overnight
- Multiple editors via remote access

## Pricing Examples
- **Basic** (edit only): €75/episode
- **Standard** (edit + transcript): €150/episode
- **Premium** (full production): €300/episode
- **Enterprise** (dedicated producer): €1500/month

## Startup Costs
- Mac Studio: €2500-4000
- Audio gear: €1000-2000
- Software: €50-100/month
- Acoustic treatment: €500-1000

## Why This Works
- Podcasting is growing massively
- Most podcasters hate editing
- AI makes production 4x faster
- Recurring revenue from retainers
- Low overhead once set up
