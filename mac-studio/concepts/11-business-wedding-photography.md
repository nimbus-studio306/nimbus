# Business Concept 11: Wedding Photography Studio

## The Business
End-to-end wedding photography service powered by AI automation.

## Hardware
- **Mac Studio M1 Max** (32GB, 512GB) - main workstation
- **External Thunderbolt SSD** (4TB) - active projects
- **NAS** (24TB+) - archive

## Revenue Streams
1. **Photography service**: €2000-5000 per wedding
2. **AI culling service**: €100-200 (for other photographers)
3. **Album design**: €200-500
4. **Print sales**: Variable markup

## AI-Powered Workflow

### Day of Wedding
- Shoot 3000-5000 images
- Backup to multiple drives on-site

### Post-Processing (AI-Assisted)
```
3000 RAW files →
  Auto-cull (reject blurry, duplicates) → 800 keepers →
  AI color grade (consistent look) →
  Face detection (group by person) →
  Export web proofs → Client selects 100 finals →
  AI-assisted retouching →
  Album layout suggestion →
  Export final deliverables
```

### Time Savings
| Task | Manual | AI-Assisted |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Culling | 4-6 hours | 30 minutes |
| Color grading | 3-4 hours | 45 minutes |
| Retouching | 2-3 hours | 1 hour |
| Album layout | 2-3 hours | 30 minutes |
| **Total** | **11-16 hours** | **~3 hours** |

## Software Stack
- **Lightroom Classic**: Core editing
- **Aftershoot/Imagen AI**: Auto-culling
- **Topaz Photo AI**: Noise reduction, sharpening
- **Affinity Photo**: Compositing
- **SmartAlbums**: Album design
- **Pixieset**: Client galleries

## Why Mac Studio?
- Handles thousands of 50MB RAW files
- Fast preview generation
- Metal-accelerated exports
- Reliable for business-critical work

## Scaling
- **Solo photographer**: 1 Mac Studio sufficient
- **Studio with associates**: Each photographer uploads, Mac processes overnight
- **White-label culling**: Offer AI culling as service to others

## Startup Costs
- Mac Studio M1 Max: €2500-4000
- External storage: €500-1000
- Software subscriptions: €100-200/month
- AI tools: €50-100/month

## Break-even
At €3000/wedding average:
- 2-3 weddings pay for hardware
- AI tools save 8+ hours per wedding
- 8 hours × €50/hour = €400 value per wedding
