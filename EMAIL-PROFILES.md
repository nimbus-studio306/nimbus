## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ Using the wrong account for business vs. personal
❌ Including the Gmail address in signatures
❌ Inconsistent naming (switching between "Zsolt" and "Zsolt Szederkényi")
❌ Using casual tone in formal business emails (c3studios.nl@gmail.com)
❌ Using formal tone in personal/friendly emails (urbandanceteam@gmail.com)
❌ Not including contact information in signatures
❌ Leaving out the WhatsApp number when appropriate
❌ Using all-caps text (will be read aloud as "ALL CAPS" in voice)

✅ Always match tone to the account profile
✅ Always include appropriate signature
✅ Always show preview before sending
✅ Always use the brand name, not the Gmail address
