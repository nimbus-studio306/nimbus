## Corrections Needed

### 1. The Lockers — Minor correction
- **Article says:** Founded 1971
- **Correction:** **1973** is more commonly cited for professional formation
- **Also:** Toni Basil (not Tony Basil) — she was already successful before joining

### 2. Electric Boogaloos — Clarification
- Boogaloo emerged in Oakland late 1960s
- **Note:** Independent of Black Panther movement (same cultural milieu, but not directly connected)
- Popping is separate style, developed later, influenced by boogaloo

### 3. Latin Symbolics — Needs verification
- **Issue:** "Latin Symbolics" not widely recognized in mainstream sources as hustle creators
- Marine Boy (Willie Estrada) and St. Mary's Park are confirmed as significant
- Hustle origins debated — multiple contributors

### 4. Rock Steady Crew — Date correction
- **Article says:** Founded 1979
- **Correction:** Founded **1977** in the Bronx

### 5. Paradise Garage — Important correction
- Michael Brody owned it ✓
- **But:** Larry Levan operated/was resident DJ (key figure!)
- Location: Greenwich Village, Manhattan
